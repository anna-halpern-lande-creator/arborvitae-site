What we do

An independent diligence firm for renewable energy investment.

Arbor Vitae Group Limited assesses solar, wind, and battery storage projects for the people who buy them, finance them, or rely on the energy they produce. We review the technical engineering, the financial structure, the legal position, the environmental performance, and the fit with the buyers who actually take the power.

Each engagement produces a structured assessment with clear findings, full data sources, and confidence levels — usable for investment committee, lender diligence, regulatory submission, or contract negotiation.

The firm operates from the United Kingdom. Engagements span the UK, Italy, Morocco, and the Caribbean.

How we work

Six dimensions of asset assessment.

Every project is scored across six dimensions. Each dimension has a defined methodology, structured outputs, and an audit trail. The combined result is an asset assessment that institutional buyers, lenders, and regulators can rely on.

Technical
How much energy the project produces, how reliably, and how long it will last. Resource modelling, equipment review, grid connection, performance over time.
Financial
Does the project make money. Revenue from contracted and market sales, costs over the asset lifetime, returns to investors and lenders.
Legal
Clean title, valid planning consent, durable contracts, no surprises. Land, permits, grid connection agreements, offtake contracts, change-of-control terms.
Environmental & Social
Carbon performance, ecological impact, community acceptance. Compliance with current and emerging environmental standards. Social licence to operate.
Machine Trust™
A new buyer class is emerging: large AI computing operations that consume electricity continuously and need specific properties from their power supply. Machine Trust scores whether a project matches what those buyers actually need.
Data & Reporting
Structured, machine-readable assessment reports with full source citations, confidence intervals, and update history. Designed for institutional use and integration with emerging digital reporting standards.
Founder

Founded and led by Anna Halpern-Lande.

Anna Halpern-Lande has invested in renewable energy infrastructure for two decades — solar, wind, and battery storage projects across the United Kingdom, Italy, Morocco, and the Caribbean. She founded Arbor Vitae Group Limited in April 2026 to extend that investment practice into an independent diligence firm.

The firm is built around the idea that asset assessment in renewable energy is becoming more important, not less, as the buyer base broadens from utilities and corporates to AI computing operators, and as new reporting and verification requirements emerge from regulators on both sides of the Atlantic.

Arbor Vitae's intellectual property includes three provisional patent applications in preparation, planned trademark registrations, and methodology documents being prepared for publication.
